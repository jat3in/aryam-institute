PROGRAMS @ARYA - CSIR NET JRF

We offer CSIR NET preparatory programs in Six subjects:

A) Intensive Contact Program

Our Intensive Contact Program is a novel and innovative program, distinguishing itself from the prevailing six-month CSIR UGC NET JRF preparatory programs across India. We stand as pioneers in offering a year-long contact program in CSIR NET JRF Physical Sciences. This program aims to empower students to excel in Theoretical, Computational, and Experimental research at esteemed Indian institutes like TIFR, ISI, ICTS, HRI, BARC, ISRO, IUCAA, etc.

B) Regular Contact Program

Our Regular Contact Program is tailored for students who have completed their MSc or are in the Third Semester and intend to clear the examination within six months. This program not only facilitates CSIR NET JRF preparation but also aids in qualifying exams like GATE, JEST, TIFR, BARC, DRDO, JNU, and other PhD entrances.

C) Chemical Sciences

We will be offering a RCP( Regular Contact Program) in Chemical Sciences as well from the coming year ie 2017 .It will be a wholesome six month long program . The courses will be offered from the list as under : CHY300:Quantum Chemistry CHY301:Spectroscopy CHY302:Structure, Bonding & Reactivity CHY303:Introduction to Mathematical Chemistry and Data Analysis CHY304:Group Theory in Chemistry CHY305: Chemical And Statistical Thermodynamics CHY306: Electrochemistry CHY307:Chemical Kinetics CHY308:Surface Chemistry CHY309: Solid state Chemistry CHY310: Polymer Chemistry CHY311:Organic Chemistry : Nomenclature CHY312:Stereochemistry and Aromaticity CHY313: Organic reactive intermediates and Organic Reaction Mechanism CHY314: Reactions, Rearrangements and Concepts in Organic Synthesis CHY315: Pericyclic reactions And Heterocyclic Compounds CHY316: Chemistry of Natural Products CHY317: Interdisciplinary Chemistry* CHY318: Inorganic Chemistry CHY319: Analytical chemistry CHY320: Bioinorganic chemistry CHY321: Nuclear chemistry CHY313:Coordination Chemistry

D) Weekend Contact Program

This is a program designed especially for those who are either working in their home town or cannot leave their home town because of some other reasons but can happily come to Udaipur on weekends. The students are provided comprehensive 10 to 12 hours classes on every Saturday and Sunday. The students get photocopy notes of any of our regular students having good handwriting. These notes help the students prepare along with the regular students at the same pace. This program has been appreciated by the students residing nearby Udaipur. They often say that this is a boon for us as without leaving their job they are able to compete strongly with the regular students and able to fulfill their dream of getting JRF or LS. This course covers only package of 6-7 courses PHY 101: Analytical Mechanics PHY 102: Quantum Mechanics –I PHY 103: Quantum Mechanics –II PHY104: Statistical Mechanics PHY105: Electromagnetic Theory PHY106: Mathematical Physics and Numerical Methods PHY119: Select Topics in Modern Physics

E) Interview Support Program

To make place into top research institutes clearing examination is not enough ,you even have to face a rigorous interview as well ,we at Arya, also provide an interview support Program for our students .It is usually a talk session , interactive enough so that , student does not face any problem while he goes to top institutes for institutes .This depends on availability of faculty as well .But we are currently offering this course in April -May for those who cleared NET in December attempt and October –Nov for those who cleared NET in June session .

F) Mathematical Sciences

In Mathematical Sciences we offer only a yearlong course called NURTURE PROGRAM which is basically a preparatory course for MSc Students .It is not a holistic NET-JRF Mathematical Science Course ,but ,it may give students a strong background of the competition so that can they can self study .With availability of faculty we will soon expand it into a proper Regular Contact Program for Mathematical Sciences like we have for Physical Sciences and Chemical Sciences . But for this academic session,we have NURTURE PROGRAM for our students . It has the following courses MAT500:Verbal and Non Verbal Reasoning MAT501:Quantative Aptitude MAT493:Linear Algebra MAT 502: Group Theory MAT 503:Ring Theory MAT 504:Basic Real Analysis MAT 505:Complex Analysis MAT505:Number Theory MAT506: Permutations and Combinations MAT507:Topology * MAT508 :Measure Theory and Integration * MAT509:Ordinary Differential Equations MAT510:Partial Differential Equations MAT511:Calculus Of Variations MAT512: Integral Equations MAT513:Numerical Analysis MAT514: Classical Mechanics STAT100:Basic Probability Theory STAT101:Probability Distributions STAT102: Descriptive Statistics STAT103: Estimation STAT104:Stochastic Process :Markov Chains ,Birth Death Process STAT105: Linear Programming Problem And Operation Research
