MLSU Entrance Examination

Mohanlal Sukhadia University (erstwhile Udaipur University) at Udaipur is a NAAC ‘A’ accredited State University established by an Act in the year 1962 to cater the needs of higher education in Southern Rajasthan with more then 2.25 Lakh Students. The University is located in Aravalli Hill Area largely dominated by tribal populations. Endowed with rich cultural heritage, natural resources and beautiful landscape, Udaipur is a world renowned tourist attraction. It conducts PGET (Post Graduate Entrance Test ) each year .We prepare students for this examination for a short time in just three months .It covers some part of syllabus of DU/MSU/BHU/CUCET .The duration of course remains from January to June each year .
