IIT - JAM

The Indian Institute of Science Bangalore (IISc) and Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs) conduct the Joint Admission Test for M.Sc. (JAM) for admission to Integrated Ph.D. Degree Programmes at IISc Bangalore and M.Sc. (Two Years), Joint M.Sc.-Ph.D., M.Sc.-Ph.D. Dual Degree, M.Sc.-M.Tech., M.Sc.-M.S.(Research)/Ph.D. Dual Degree and other Post-Bachelor's Degree Programmes at IITs. The main objective of JAM is to consolidate Science as a career option for bright students across the country. It is expected that the research infrastructure, the inter-disciplinary interaction and the vibrant academic environment of IISc Bangalore and IITs will motivate these students to pursue R&D careers in frontier areas of basic sciences as well as inter-disciplinary areas of science and technology. We offer IITJAM preparatory Courses in the following subjects in Mathematics, Physics ,Chemistry, Statistics and Biology . We run two year program as well as one year program .Two year program is for BSc First year to second year moving and One year program is for Third year students . Two year Regular Contact Program is again a novel program in all Rajasthan . This program is for First Year to Second year moving students .The duration of course is two years .Our endeavor is to make students of other examinations like NBHM/NGPE/KVPY/RMO/ISI/TIFR/JEST . The vision is even to provide orientation and training to students to get into good research institutes like IISER /TIFR /CMI/ IMSc/ HRI /IOMA. One Year Regular Contact Program is for BSc Final Year Students designed for IITJAM entrance specifically .In our first year ,IIT Roorkee and IIEST Shibhpur have become the first beneficiary from this program .Our endeavor is to produce more talented minds and send them to IITs for Higher Academics.
